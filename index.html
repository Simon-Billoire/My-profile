Welcome to my page. I’m a curious student with a soft spot for fashion—outfits, editorials, and the way a silhouette or a colour can say something before you speak. I love following what designers do next and sharing finds with friends who get it.

Fashion & personal style

Putting looks together is half instinct, half experiment—mixing textures, playing with proportions, and knowing when less is more. I follow brands and independent labels alike, and I’m always curious about how people dress in different cities.

Runways, drops & culture

Shows, capsule collections, and the conversation around fashion online keep me hooked. I like understanding the story behind a collection—references, music, casting—and how streetwear and luxury keep influencing each other.

Visual mood & creativity

Editorials, campaign imagery, and a well-framed fit pic all matter to me. I’m drawn to strong art direction, whether it’s in a magazine, on a mood board, or in the wild—anything that blends aesthetics with identity.
